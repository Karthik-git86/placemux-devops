# PlaceMux On-Call Schedule

## Rotation
- Week 1: DevOps Engineer — Primary
- Week 2: Backend Engineer — Primary
- Week 3: Frontend Engineer — Primary

## Response Times
- P1 Production down:   15 minutes
- P2 Degraded service:  1 hour
- P3 Minor issue:       Next business day

## Escalation Path
1. Primary on-call (15 min)
2. Secondary on-call (15 min)
3. Engineering Manager (15 min)
4. CTO

## Weekly Handoff
Every Monday 9 AM — outgoing briefs incoming on:
- Open issues
- Scheduled maintenance
- Known fragile areas
